Media & Press

Members of the press, media houses, bloggers, and content creators are invited to cover the Gurukul Utsav. For press passes, media kits, interviews with organizers, or any other media-related inquiries, please reach out to our media relations team. We are committed to providing all necessary resources to facilitate comprehensive and engaging coverage of our event. We believe that widespread media coverage will help us share the spiritual and cultural richness of the Utsav with a broader audience.

Email: media@gurukulutsav.org

Our media team is available to assist with requests during business hours, Monday to Friday. Please provide details about your organization and your specific request to help us process it efficiently. We look forward to collaborating with you to highlight the unique aspects of Gurukul Utsav and its profound impact on the community. Advanced notice is appreciated for interview requests to ensure availability of key personnel.

We also welcome suggestions for unique storytelling angles or special access requests that can enhance your coverage. Our goal is to make it as easy as possible for the media to capture the essence of the Utsav.
